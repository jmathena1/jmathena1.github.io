Hi, I'm John and I like to write. Thanks for coming to my site. I live in Baltimore City with my lovely wife Courtney, our dog Kermit, and two cats Bruce and Hank.

My poetry and stories often come from memorable experiences or vivid dreams I remember to jot down and return to when I have time to process them. I've written about everything from an abandoned zoo in Morocco, a peace walk I attended in my city, and a dream where the devil trained me to be a demon.

Professionally, I work as a data analyst. I use my portfolio page to flesh out any interesting ideas and showcase existing work. I'm a big fan of government open data portals and public data so I have a list of Baltimore City and State of Maryland datasets I plan to go over.

If you have any questions or wanna reach out to chat about something, please do so! You can send me an email at jmathena24@gmail.com or reach out through some of the social media links at the bottom.
