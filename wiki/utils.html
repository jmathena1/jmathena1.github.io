### Utils - utils is short for utilities and i believe programmers create utils files to store code that they will use throughout a piece of software. helps us stay DRY (don't repeat yourself) - but i'm also using it here to just mean things i find useful. might fit the definition above too. and it might not https://regex101.com/ - regular expressions (regex) are code that represent a pattern. you can search a text (string or group of strings in programming parlance) for a pattern you construct to help you extract text you need from a large document or series of documents. - the regex tester is helpful because you can paste any text in it, create a pattern to test, and the tester will tell you exactly what your pattern picks up. a huge time saver
